About Me

I'm Kevin, a self-taught developer from Kisumu, Kenya, dedicated to using AI and technology to bridge the technological divide. Growing up in an underserved community, I’ve seen firsthand the barriers to socio-economic uplifting posed by language and access to the digital world. My mission is to create inclusive solutions, like my Sheng-Dholuo translator and an e-commerce platform - projects that I envision will empower local communities.

With expertise in Python, Java, PHP, typescript and Node.js, and frameworks like Django, I’ve built projects that resonate with real-world needs. I’m excited to contribute to initiatives like AI for African languages, bringing my skills and community insights to drive impact.
